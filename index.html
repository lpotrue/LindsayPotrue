Horse Express

Horse Express was designed with the idea of tracking the exercise schedule for horses in training. Designed for amature riders and professionals alike. The convenience of taking notes is right at your finger tips. Rate the progress of your training and keep an up to date schedule with Horse Express. The app features file uploads through Amazon Web Services to allow the user to upload a profile photo of their horse/ horses. Users are also able to signup and create a profile for each of their horses, all under one account. Connect with fellow equestrians to view the progress and comments about other horses.
